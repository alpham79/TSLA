TSLA 2014


The Texas Surplus Lines Association, Inc., the TSLA, is a non-profit, trade association of excess and surplus lines brokerage and underwriting professionals. The TSLA membership includes insurance carriers and industry vendors, as well.

The TSLA provides legislative, regulatory, educational and networking opportunities for their membership. .


TSLA Mission Statement

To promote the general business well-being of its membership; and

To inform and be an advocate for our members and stakeholders regarding the Excess & Surplus Lines market by communicating and disseminating information about our segment of the industry and its role in the insurance marketplace as a whole, and

To encourage compliance with the general provisions of the Texas Insurance Code, as they relate to excess and surplus lines insurance.
