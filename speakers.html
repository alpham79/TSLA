Speakers Program

TSLA 2014

    "Market Update"
    Art Davis, President, Colony Specialty Group
    Mr. Davis will provide the latest market update information

    "We've Got You Covered: Avoiding E&O Pitfalls"
    Richard Gateley, Attorney, Brackett & Ellis
    One in seven TSLA members will likely face an E&O claim-probably from a lawsuit after a loss. What can members do to minimize their exposure?

    "Dazzling Blue - The Journey from Ordinary to Extraordinary"
    Steve Harvill, Creative Ventures
    DAZZLING BLUE is the latest strategic platform from Stephen Harvill and Creative Ventures. With DAZZLING BLUE you will be immersed in a multimedia experience specifically designed to provide you with a simple three part plan to start you on the journey to extraordinary.

    "NAPSLO Update on State, National and Federal Issues"
    Brady Kelly, Executive Director, NAPSLO
    NAPSLO Executive Director, Brady Kelley, will provide important updates on legislative and regulatory policy developments at the state, national and federal level. NAPSLO's update will include highlights from the December 2013 report of the Federal Insurance Office (FIO), How to Modernize and Improve the System of Insurance Regulation in the United States, and the January 2014 report of the Government Accountability Office (GAO), Effects of the Nonadmitted and Reinsurance Reform Act of 2010.

    Association updates will be provided by:
    Kevin Westrope, NAPSLO
